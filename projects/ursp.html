URSP Blade Machining Project

This project aims to enhance the surface integrity of aero-engine blades by integrating cooled air minimum quantity lubrication (CAMQL) technology with the low-plasticity ultrasonic rolling strengthening process (URSP) on a customized 5-axis CNC platform.
